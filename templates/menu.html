🌿 Plant Care Made Simple

💧

Watering Guide

Most plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Check the soil with your finger!

☀️

Light Requirements

Bright indirect light works for most houseplants. Avoid direct sunlight on leaves.

🌡️

Temperature & Humidity

Room temperature (65-75°F) and 40-60% humidity keep most plants happy.
